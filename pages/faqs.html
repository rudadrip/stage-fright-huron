Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most commonly asked questions here. If you need further assistance, don't hesitate to reach out.

1. How long will it take for my product to arrive?

Depending on current product stocks and purchase time, it may take up to 2 weeks for us to deliver your goods. If you believe your order is taking longer than usual to arrive, you should call our shipping provider at +1 (517)-123-4567 or visit their tracking website at www.mailtracking.com, be sure to have your order confirmation number ready!

2. How can I return a product I have purchased?

If you wish to return a product for any reason you may do so provided that certain criteria are met. These vary by product type and can be found below.
Clothing/Merchandise
Clothing and other related merchandise items can be returned within 30 days of their purchase, the product(s) must not be damaged or altered in any way for your return to be accepted. If returned items are found to be in unsatisfactory conditions they will not be returned to you and no refund will be issued.
Music
Music of any form, digital or analog, may be returned for a full refund within 14 days of being purchased. If a physical music medium is returned to the warehouse damaged, half or no refund will be given depending on the severity of the damage.
Tour Tickets
Tickets for most tours can be returned before 24 hours of the start time for a full refund, those returned within 24 of a performance will be issued a half refund. Certain performances may have special return policies in effect, these policies, if present, will be displayed at the top of the venue overview page.

3. I lost my ticket, what can I do?

All purchased tickets, digital or physical, are accompanied by a confirmation message and ID sent via email or text message. These can be shown to the help desk or ticket counter to receive a replacement ticket. In the event that you do not have this confirmation message for any reason you may contact our online support or a help desk at a performance for assistance. To make the process easier please ensure you have the billing information used for your purchase ready.

4. Are there discounts for bulk purchases?

Generally we do not offer discounts for bulk purchases, however, very significant orders may receive a discount determined on a case-by-case basis.

5. Are pre-orders available for upcoming products?

We do not offer pre-orders for upcoming music releases but upcoming clothing and accessories are generally available for pre-orders.

6. How do I check if my ticket is real?

If you bought your ticket from this website or from a verified vendor there is no need to be worried since these tickets will always be legitimate. Tickets obtained from other sources can be inspected for an ID number, the correct date, time, and location, and a valid section and seat for the ticket type. If all of these are correct a ticket is more likely legitimate than false but this is not guaranteed and we cannot ensure you will be admitted.

7. What happens to my ticket if a concert is cancelled or moved?

If a concert gets cancelled or moved there are a few options available depending on what happened. If a concert was moved all tickets for the original date will still be valid, if you cannot make the new date you may contact customer service to either exchange it for an equivalent ticket for another performance, or exchange it for credit that can be used on other Stage Fright products. If a concert is cancelled all tickets will be immediately converted to credit but you may request it be replaced by a cash refund by contacting customer service.

8. What music formats are available?

All of our albums and singles are available on Blu-ray, vinyl, and digital media. They're also available on all major music streaming platforms (Spotify, YouTube, iTunes, etc.)

1. What are the differences between ticket/seat types?

Tickets come in different tiers, each offering increasing benefits such as early entry, exclusive merchandise, and VIP meet-and-greets.

2. What time do doors open for performances?

Doors generally open 2-3 hours before the start of a performance, however, some services may be unavailable during this time.

3. Are the venues accessible for people with disabilities?

We cannot guarantee each performance will be accessible due to variations in venue design but we strive to include everyone and will do our best to provide services for such people.

4. Are the performances age restricted?

No, performances are not age restricted, however, songs may include explicit words so parental discretion is advised.

5. Is there parking available at the venues?

Yes, parking is available at all venues, however, some venues may require a further walk from the parking area to the performance than other venues.

6. Is recording allowed?

No, recording of a performance is not allowed at any time. Pictures are permitted though. Stop looking at your phone and enjoy the music!

7. What items are allowed in the venue?

Specific items generally vary venue to venue but a general guideline is that any and all weapons are not allowed, bags must be clear or smaller than a hand purse, and all external food products are not allowed. If you're ever in doubt if an item is allowed or not, it's generally wise to not bring it.

8. Can I meet the band members after a performance?

Generally there's a meet and greet after performances where you can get autographs and meet members but this may not be available for every performance
